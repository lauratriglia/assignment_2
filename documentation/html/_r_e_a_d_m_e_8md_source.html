1 # Research Track I - Final Assignment
2 
3 In this repository you will find a package required for the construction of an interface whose the scope is to take commands from the user and execute them.
4 The possible commands are:
5 1) Reach a random target
6 2) Choose a target among some possibilities which are (-4,-3);(-4,2);(-4,7);(5,-7);(5,-3);(5,1)
7 3) Follow the wall
8 4) Stop the robot
9
